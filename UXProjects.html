What is Human Computer Interaction?

SUNY Oswego Human Computer Interaction (click me)

These disciplines can go from computer science and programming different languages such as C# and javascript, to user experience research and design (UX/UI), all the way to psychology where we can study computer science on more of a neuroscience aspect. I am currently researching game UX, virtual reality, and usability testing in a few of my classes.
