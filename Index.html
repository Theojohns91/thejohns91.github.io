I am a Human Computer Interation (HCI) graduate student at State University of New York at Oswego. I have my undergraduate degree in Psychology with a minor in Cognitive Science. My past work involves biometric studies involving human behavior patterns, along with Virtual Reality and Game User Experience. My future goal is to become a User Experience Researcher involving Virtual Reality and Game Usability Testing.

I am experienced in academic writing and have published a study in video game user experience. Currently, I am working as a graduate assistant in human computer interaction. During this current semester, I am also running studies in virtual reality UX and studying usability testing with various video games.

In my spare time, I enjoy bowling, playing and writing music, fitness, and attending informative seminars for the computer science department.
